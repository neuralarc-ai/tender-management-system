## ğŸš¨ What If Something Goes Wrong?

### "Document parsing service not configured"
**What it means:** The AI service needs to be set up by your IT admin

**What to do:** Contact your system administrator or IT support

### Low Confidence Score (< 60%)
**What it means:** The AI couldn't extract some information clearly

**What to do:**
1. Click "Review & Edit"
2. Manually fill in any missing or incorrect fields
3. Submit normally

### Deadline Not Found
**What it means:** No clear deadline in the uploaded documents

**What to do:**
1. You'll see a warning message
2. Manually enter the submission deadline
3. All other fields remain auto-filled
4. Submit normally

### Some Fields Show "[Auto-extracted - Please review and complete]"
**What it means:** That specific information wasn't found in the document

**What to do:**
1. Click "Review & Edit"
2. Fill in those specific fields manually
3. Other fields remain auto-filled
4. Submit
