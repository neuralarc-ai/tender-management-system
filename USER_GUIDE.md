## üë• For Clients (TCS/DCS)

### Step 1: Submit a Tender

1. Navigate to the **Client Portal**
2. Click the **"Submit New Tender"** button (top right)
3. Fill in all required fields:
   - **Tender Title:** Give your project a clear, descriptive name
   - **Detailed Description:** Explain what you need in detail
   - **Scope of Work:** Define deliverables and project boundaries
   - **Technical Requirements:** List technologies, platforms, integrations
   - **Functional Requirements:** Describe features and capabilities
   - **Submission Deadline:** Set when you need proposals by
   - **Eligibility Criteria:** Specify vendor qualifications
   - **Supporting Documents:** Upload any relevant files (optional)

4. Click **"Submit Tender"**

### Step 2: Monitor Your Tender

- Your tender appears immediately in the "Active Tenders" tab
- Watch the **countdown timer** showing time remaining until deadline
- The system automatically triggers AI analysis in the admin portal

### Step 3: Review Proposals

When Neural Arc submits a proposal:

1. You'll see a **"Action Required"** notification on the tender card
2. Click **"Review Proposal"** button
3. Review the proposal details:
   - Executive Summary
   - Understanding of Requirements
   - Technical Approach
   - Scope Coverage
   - Delivery Timeline
   - Commercial Details

4. Provide optional feedback in the text box
5. Click either:
   - **"Accept Proposal"** - Approve the proposal
   - **"Reject Proposal"** - Decline the proposal

### Step 4: Track Status

- **Draft:** Proposal is being prepared
- **Submitted:** Proposal awaits your review
- **Under Review:** You're reviewing the proposal
- **Accepted:** You've approved the proposal ‚úì
- **Rejected:** You've declined the proposal ‚úó

## üß† For Admins (Neural Arc Inc)

### Step 1: View Available Tenders

1. Navigate to the **Admin Portal**
2. See all tenders in the left sidebar
3. Click any tender to view details

### Step 2: Review Tender Details

**Overview Tab:**
- Read complete tender requirements
- Review all sections:
  - Description
  - Scope of Work
  - Technical Requirements
  - Functional Requirements
  - Eligibility Criteria
  - Supporting Documents

### Step 3: Monitor AI Analysis

**AI Analysis Tab:**

Wait 5-10 seconds for analysis to complete. You'll see:

**Scores:**
- **Relevance Score:** How well the tender matches our capabilities
- **Feasibility Score:** How deliverable the project is
- **Overall Score:** Combined assessment

**Delivery Breakdown:**
- **Fully Deliverable:** What we can completely deliver
- **Partially Deliverable:** What needs clarification or phased approach
- **Out of Scope:** What's beyond our capabilities

**Insights:**
- **Gaps:** Missing information or unclear requirements
- **Risks:** Potential challenges or concerns
- **Assumptions:** What we're assuming about the project

### Step 4: Edit Proposal Draft

**Proposal Draft Tab:**

The system automatically generates a draft proposal. You can:

1. **Review AI-Generated Content:**
   - Executive Summary
   - Understanding of Requirements
   - Technical Approach
   - Scope Coverage
   - Exclusions
   - Assumptions
   - Delivery Timeline

2. **Edit Any Section:**
   - Click in any text area
   - Modify content as needed
   - Add your expertise and insights

3. **Add Commercial Details:**
   - Pricing structure
   - Payment terms
   - Milestones
   - Any other commercial information

4. **Save Your Work:**
   - Click **"Save Draft"** to save changes
   - You can return and edit anytime before submission

### Step 5: Submit Proposal

1. Review all sections one final time
2. Ensure commercial details are complete
3. Click **"Submit Proposal to Client"**
4. Confirm submission
5. Proposal is instantly sent to client portal

### Step 6: Track Proposal Status

- **Draft:** Still being prepared
- **Submitted:** Sent to client, awaiting review
- **Under Review:** Client is reviewing
- **Accepted:** Client approved! ‚úì
- **Rejected:** Client declined

2. **Customize Proposals:** Don't just submit the AI draft - add your expertise

4. **Be Transparent:** Clearly state what's in and out of scope
5. **Add Value:** Include insights and recommendations beyond requirements

## üìä Understanding AI Scores

### Score Interpretation

**80-100% (High):**
- Excellent match with capabilities
- High confidence in delivery
- Minimal risks identified

**60-79% (Medium):**
- Good match with some gaps
- Deliverable with clarifications
- Moderate risks to manage

**Below 60% (Low):**
- Significant gaps or misalignment
- May require partnerships or additional resources
- Higher risk profile

### Delivery Breakdown

**Example:**
- **85% Fully Deliverable:** Core requirements we can completely handle
- **10% Partially Deliverable:** Features needing clarification or phased approach
- **5% Out of Scope:** Specialized requirements beyond our expertise

## üö® Troubleshooting

### Tender Not Appearing?
- Wait 2 seconds for sync
- Check you're on the correct portal
- Refresh the page if needed

### AI Analysis Stuck?
- Analysis takes 5-10 seconds
- If stuck longer, refresh the page
- Check the AI Analysis tab

### Can't Submit Proposal?
- Ensure all required fields are filled
- Save draft first
- Check for error messages

### File Upload Issues?
- Maximum file size: 10MB
- Supported formats: PDF, DOC, DOCX, XLS, XLSX
- Try uploading one file at a time
